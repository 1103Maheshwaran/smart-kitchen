Introduction to Smart Kitchen

Smart Kitchen is an IOt based kitchen project to reduce
the work load for the people working
in kitchen

Abstract

The Project Aims at developing an IOt platform to record ananlyde and notify
people regarding their diet and aldo enables automatic purchace
through their prefered vendor via mail or message

Features

IOt based platform enables friendly extension to analyse further kitchen commodities.
Saperate mailer script also helps
automatic purchase of commodities through prefered vendor
